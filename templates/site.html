Introduction

Welcome to Hope, an academic project developed by a group of final year students from department of Computer Science & Technology at A.P.C. Ray Polytechnic. Our goal was to create a simple and intuitive stock price predictor that showcases the potential of deep learning in finance. We chose to focus on stock price prediction as it is a complex and intriguing problem that has garnered significant attention in recent years. We chose to make Hope free and accessible to the public, not for business or financial gain, but to satisfy curiosity. We understand that our predictor is not accurate and should not be used for making investment decisions. Instead, we hope that Hope will serve as a tool for those interested in understanding the basics of stock market prediction and the potential of machine learning in finance. By providing a free and interactive platform, we aim to inspire curiosity and showcase our skills and knowledge as students.
